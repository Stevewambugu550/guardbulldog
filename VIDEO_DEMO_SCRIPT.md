### **Scene 2: Automated Email Analysis (Guest User)**

**[00:30-01:30]**

**Visual:** Navigate to the "Email Analyzer" page. Copy and paste a sample suspicious email (with headers) into the text area.

**Narration:**
"A key problem we identified is that most users don't know what makes an email suspicious. To solve this, we've created a powerful Automated Email Analyzer that requires no login and no technical expertise."

"A user can simply copy the entire suspicious email, including the technical headers, and paste it into this single text box. Let's use a sample phishing email to demonstrate."

**(Action: Paste email content)**

"Now, I'll click 'Analyze Email'. The system instantly parses the headers and content, checking for multiple threat indicators like suspicious URLs, sender authentication issues, and high-pressure language."

**Visual:** Show the analysis results page, pointing out the risk score, threat level, and detailed breakdown.

**Narration:**
"As you can see, the analysis is complete. The email has been flagged as 'Phishing' with a high-risk score. The results provide a clear recommendation and a detailed breakdown of the indicators found, such as a sender domain mismatch and the use of suspicious keywords. This empowers any user to make an informed decision without needing to be a security expert."
