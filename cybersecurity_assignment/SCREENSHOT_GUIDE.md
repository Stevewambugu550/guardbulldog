## How to Take Screenshots on Windows

### Method 1: Snipping Tool (Recommended)
1. Press **Windows Key + Shift + S**
2. Screen dims - select area to capture
3. Screenshot copies to clipboard
4. Open Paint or Word
5. Press **Ctrl + V** to paste
6. Save with descriptive name

### Method 2: Full Screen
1. Press **PrtScn** (Print Screen) key
2. Opens Snipping Tool
3. Save screenshot

### Method 3: Snip & Sketch
1. Press **Windows Key + Shift + S**
2. Choose rectangular, freeform, window, or fullscreen
3. Screenshot saved to clipboard
4. Notification appears - click to edit/save

## Troubleshooting

### "I can't access Google Search Console"
**Solution:** Just screenshot the login page - it shows the tool exists. Or use the "Remove Outdated Content" tool instead which doesn't require login.

### "Facebook won't let me see certain settings"
**Solution:** Screenshot what you CAN see. The assignment just needs to show you understand the process.

### "Website is down or changed"
**Solution:** Use alternative sites:
- Instead of PrivacyDuck: Use Abine.com or OneRep.com

- Document which alternative you used

### "Screenshot is too large"
