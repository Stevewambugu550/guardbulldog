# Quick Start: Get Your Kali Linux Screenshot in 30 Minutes

## Fastest Path to Complete Your Assignment

## Step 1: Download VirtualBox (5 minutes)
1. Open your web browser
2. Go to: **https://www.virtualbox.org/wiki/Downloads**
3. Click **"Windows hosts"**
4. Run the downloaded installer
5. Click "Next" through all prompts (keep defaults)

---

## Step 2: Download Kali Linux (15-30 minutes depending on internet)
1. Go to: **https://www.kali.org/get-kali/#kali-virtual-machines**
2. Find the **"VirtualBox"** section
3. Click **"64-bit (OVA)"** download button
4. Save to your Downloads folder
5. Wait for download to complete (~3.5 GB)

**While downloading:** You can work on other things, this takes time

---

## Step 3: Import Kali into VirtualBox (5 minutes)
1. Open **VirtualBox** application
2. Click **File** ‚Üí **Import Appliance**

7. Click **Import** (don't change settings)
8. Wait 5-10 minutes for import

## Step 4: Start Kali Linux (2 minutes)

## Step 5: Take Your Screenshot (1 minute)

### Method 1: Windows Snipping Tool
1. Press **Windows Key + Shift + S**
2. Click and drag to select the Kali VM window
3. Screenshot is copied to clipboard
4. Open **Paint** or **Word**
5. Press **Ctrl + V** to paste
6. Save as: **kali_vm_screenshot.png**

### Method 2: Make It Look Professional (Optional)
Before taking screenshot:
1. In Kali, click the terminal icon (top left, black square)
2. Type: `neofetch`
3. Press Enter
4. Now take screenshot - shows system info with Kali logo!

## Troubleshooting

### "VT-x is not available" Error
**Fix:** Enable virtualization in BIOS
1. Restart your computer
2. Press **F2** or **Del** during startup (watch for "Press F2 for Setup")
3. Find "Virtualization" or "Intel VT-x" setting
4. Change to **Enabled**
5. Save and Exit (usually F10)

### VM is Very Slow
**Fix:** Give it more resources
1. Close the VM (if running)
2. Right-click Kali in VirtualBox
3. Click **Settings**
4. Go to **System** tab
5. Increase **Base Memory** to 2048 MB or more
6. Go to **Processor** tab
7. Increase CPUs to 2
8. Click OK

### Download is Taking Forever
- Use a wired connection if possible
- Download overnight
- Or use university WiFi (usually faster)
